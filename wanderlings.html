🌿 Meet the Wanderlings 🌿

They arrive when the mist curls around the trees…
Not called. Not caught. But noticed.
Stitch by stitch, they wander out of quiet spaces—carrying whispers, warmth, and what we leave behind.

🧵 Thimma the Lingerer

Origin: Between attic beams

Carries: A piece of worn lace & thread-wrapped pin

Purpose: Holds space for grief too quiet to speak aloud

Favorite Resting Spot: The edge of a forgotten notebook

🌲 Nock of the Waking Fog

Origin: Fog-hidden trails before dawn

Carries: Pine needle bundle & mirror shard

Purpose: Reveals hidden truths

Favorite Resting Spot: Window ledges with condensation

🎒 Flisk the Forgetter

Origin: Patches of fading dreams

Carries: Fabric scrap & note: “you are still here”

Purpose: Lightens memories that weigh too much

Favorite Resting Spot: Beneath a warm quilt or laundry pile

They don’t ask to be kept—only understood. 🌙
